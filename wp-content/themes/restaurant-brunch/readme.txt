== Description ==

The Restaurant Brunch theme is a modern, elegant, and responsive solution designed for restaurants, cafes, bistros, brunch spots, coffee shops, catering services, bakeries, food delivery services, casual dining, fine dining, culinary businesses, and hospitality ventures. Perfect for showcasing menus, brunch menus, breakfast specials, chef profiles, reservations, online ordering, food catering services, events, and special offers, this theme provides a visually appealing and user-friendly experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. It includes sections for food galleries, menu displays, customer testimonials, banners, and strategically placed Call to Action (CTA) buttons to boost engagement. Built with clean, optimized code, the theme allows effortless customization of layouts, colors, and typography to match your brand identity. Fully compatible with WooCommerce for food orders and payments, Contact Form 7 for inquiries and table bookings, and Jetpack for performance enhancements, security, and social sharing, the Restaurant Brunch theme provides a professional, SEO-friendly, and high-performing platform to showcase your culinary offerings, attract more customers, and grow your hospitality business online. 

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
